Welcome to Punjab College of Technical Education

Nurturing Students Into Leaders Since 1999

Located in the welcoming state of Punjab in India, the PCTE Group of Institutes is a premier educational institution that focuses on developing the practical as well as theoretical skills of its students. The college offers various courses in different backgrounds like Engineering, Information Technology, Pharmacy, Journalism, Biotechnology, Medical Lab Technology, management, hospitality, fashion designing, commerce, etc. Students from across the globe have witnessed the serenity and exposure that the college has to offer and have had made some life long memories being here. PCTE is affiliated with IK Gujral Punjab Technical University and is well known for its cultural events and programmes like Koshish, Ehsaas, Turf, and many more co-curricular activities that are held almost every month. Established in 1999, PCTE is ranked as the best B School in North India today, all thanks to its skilled staff, state-of-the-art infrastructure, exciting competitions, events, and various entrepreneurial opportunities that are given to the students.
